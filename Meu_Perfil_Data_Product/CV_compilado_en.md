Currently I am a Domain Specialist at Grupo Boticário, where I work on data visualization, data modeling, and machine learning projects. I actively participated in the transition from QlikView (SAP Hana) to a democratized data standard, using Tableau and Looker (Google BigQuery) in a modern and scalable data ecosystem.
